About Me

My name is Sergio Martino and I am Frontend Software Engineer from Italy, living in Hertfordshire, UK.

I have worked for major financial groups such as Deutsche Bank, CMC Markets, IG, Société Générale and contributed to the design and development of web applications consumed by hundreds of thousands of people.

I am not a tech geek. I am passionate about products, User Experience and real-world technology that makes sense in the context.

When I’m not coding, I enjoy playing my Kawai piano and basketball.
